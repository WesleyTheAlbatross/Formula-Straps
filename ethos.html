Our Mission

Engineered
by Obsession.

Precision is not an accident. It is the result of thousands of iterations, obsessive material sourcing, and a refusal to compromise on the smallest detail.

At Formula Strap, we believe your wristwear should carry the same DNA as the machines that dominate the grid. We source only the finest Grade A Italian leathers, renowned for their durability and their ability to develop a rich, unique patina over time.

Every single strap is individually cut and hand-stitched. The circuit layouts are not printed; they are micron-etched using high-frequency lasers, ensuring that the apexes of Monaco and the sweeping curves of Monza are captured with absolute mathematical perfection.

The Material

Hand-selected Italian calfskin, tanned with vegetable extracts for a carbon-neutral finish.

The Craft

Hand-stitched in our UK studio using reinforced nautical-grade thread.
