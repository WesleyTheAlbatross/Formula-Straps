Our Ethos

Precision is not an accident. It is the result of obsessive engineering, thousands of iterations, and a refusal to compromise on materials.

At Formula Strap, we believe your wristwear should carry the same DNA as the machines that dominate the grid. We source only the finest Italian vegetable-tanned leather, renowned for its durability and its ability to develop a rich, unique patina over time—just like the steering wheel of a classic 1970s racer.

Every single strap is individually cut and hand-stitched in our workshop. The circuit layouts are not printed; they are micron-etched using state-of-the-art CO2 lasers, ensuring that the apexes of Monaco and the sweeping curves of Silverstone are captured with absolute, mathematical perfection.
